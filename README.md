### Project Overview
This project analyzes the key factors influencing bike purchasing decisions. The goal is to identify patterns in customer demographics, income levels, and lifestyle to help the business optimize its marketing efforts.

![Dashboard Screenshot](https://github.com/AntoinetteSa/Excel-Bike-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Dashboard%20Sales%20of%20bikes.png)

### Key Stages
* **Data Cleaning:** Removed duplicates and standardized "Marital Status" (Married/Single) and "Gender" (Male/Female) using **Power Query**. Created a conditional **Age Group** column for better segmentation.
* **Data Analysis:** Built Pivot Tables to calculate average income, commute distance, and purchase counts.
* **Visualization:** Developed an interactive dashboard with Slicers for Region, Education, and Gender. Created custom infographics for gender distribution.

### Key Insights
* **Income:** Bike buyers have a higher average income ($57,963) compared to non-buyers ($54,875).
* **Distance:** The highest purchasing activity is among customers living within **0-1 miles** of work (200 units sold).
* **Demographics:** Demand is nearly equal between men (51.1%) and women (48.9%), suggesting product versatility.

### Business Recommendations
* Focus marketing efforts on customers living within a 1-mile radius of sales points.
* Target premium bike lines at the "Adults" age group with above-average income levels.

### Tech Stack
* **Microsoft Excel:** Power Query, Pivot Tables, Advanced Charting, Dashboard Design.
